👁️ Computer Vision
Surgical Action Recognition — IVT Triplet Classification & Segmentation
CholecT50 · Jan 2026 – Present
+

Teaching a model to watch laparoscopic surgery and recognise exactly what's happening — which instrument, doing which action, to which anatomy — frame by frame.

  • Built a ResNet-50 multi-label classifier on CholecT50 with separate IVT heads — 6 instruments, 10 verbs, 100 triplet classes — trained with Binary Cross-Entropy loss.
  • Engineered a Gaussian spatial attention mechanism grounded in bounding-box geometry, anchoring the model's focus onto actual instrument locations.
  • Achieved +21.5% triplet IVT mAP and +7.9% instrument mAP vs. CAM-based feature pooling, through geometry-informed spatial grounding.

Claude Mumbai Hackathon
+

About me
